## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### For Pitching to Investors
- Lead with the **problem**: 85M+ Americans struggle with allergies, 37M have diabetes
- Show the **market size**: $1.8B serviceable addressable market
- Demonstrate **traction**: User surveys, waitlist signups, beta testers
- Highlight **competitive advantage**: Only app combining allergies + diabetes
- Present **clear path to revenue**: Premium subscriptions, projected $280K Year 1

### For Building Your MVP
- **Start small**: Focus on scanner + shopping list only
- **Test early**: Get in users' hands within 2-3 months
- **Iterate fast**: Weekly updates based on feedback
- **Don't over-engineer**: Use third-party APIs for data
- **Mobile first**: Most users will be in stores

### For User Acquisition
- **Community-first**: Engage in diabetes/allergy forums before launching
- **Partner strategically**: Work with FARE, ADA, JDRF for credibility
- **Content marketing**: Blog posts, YouTube tutorials, social media tips
- **Word of mouth**: Make sharing easy with referral rewards
- **Healthcare channel**: Get doctors to recommend your app
