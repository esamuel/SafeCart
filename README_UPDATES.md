## ğŸ’¡ Why This Approach?

### Benefits of Starting Private:

1. **Health Data Protection**
   - Allergies, diabetes, and medical conditions are sensitive
   - Users feel safe sharing health struggles privately
   - Reduced legal liability (HIPAA, GDPR compliance easier)

2. **Trust Building**
   - Closed community builds trust faster
   - All users have similar health needs
   - Quality, authentic content without external pressure

3. **User Comfort**
   - People more willing to share health journeys in private space
   - No public scrutiny or judgment
   - Safe space for vulnerable discussions

4. **Quality Over Quantity**
   - Focused, supportive community
   - Less spam and trolls
   - Meaningful engagement

### When to Add External Integration:

- Users explicitly request it
